About

Inspiration

Waste Disposal is a huge problem that affects climate change directly. It's estimated that around 55 percent of global plastic waste was discarded, 25 percent was incinerated, and only 20 percent was recycled. The wanton disposal of plastic led us to think of an incentive-based system.

What is Garbage Buddy?

Waste Disposal is a huge problem that affects climate change directly. It's estimated that around 55 percent of global plastic waste was discarded, 25 percent was incinerated, and only 20 percent was recycled. Now our project aims to inspire people, give them a certain incentive in order to recycle their garbage.

How we built it

We used a MQ135 gas sensor with a ultrasonic sensor, a neo-6m gps module and a nodemcu.
